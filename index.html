Evaluation systems

1,000+ hours evaluating models — here's what "evaluation" means in practice.

Illustrative scoring profile across the dimensions used in professional model evaluation work.

  • Reasoning quality — does the model's chain of logic hold up, not just its final answer.
  • Accuracy — factual correctness against ground truth and source material.
  • Instruction adherence — did the model do what was actually asked, including constraints.
  • Naturalness — does the output read like something a thoughtful person would write or say.
  • Human-likeness — for voice and audio models, does delivery match human conversational cadence.
